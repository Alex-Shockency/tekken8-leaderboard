Where can I make suggestions or report bugs?

This is a Work In Progress! If you have any suggestions for the site, please let us know! We can gather your feedback as necessary.

I just got a promotion why is my rank still the old one?

If your rank is not up-to-date, it might be because it hasn't recorded any games after you've reached your last rank! In order for the .png to update after a rank change, you must play at least one game before the site will recognize that you are at that updated rank!

What does it mean to be qualified?

Qualified just means you've played 30 games in the last 30 days on a given character.

What is the All-time tab?

This records the highest GLICKO rating that you've obtained with a given character.

What even is this rating?

This is your Tekken 8 GLICKO rating, obtained from your Ranked Match history. For more information, please Google it.
